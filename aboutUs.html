About Us

Welcome to Keshen, your number one source for recipes from where and whenever. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of experience since food is our passion too, with a focus on quality, taste and actual ingredients you can find. We at Clutch had this idea of making a community of great people with a shared passion, hence the birth of Keshen, Keshen first started out as a simple project and will be something of a marvel in the future, and our passion for food or at least our appetites' will not grow old for we are food lovers helping other food lovers be more friendly, providing the best recipes for our fellow enthusiasts. we were driven by the implementation of this project of ours and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online community. We now serve recipes all over the place from the homeland "Injera " to the incredibly hard to make beef wellington, and are thrilled to be a part of this future and will work our very best till our last breathe to bring you the best recipes that we can find. For our job is making you happy we have ways where you can interact and help others with the recipes they need.
We hope you enjoy our recipes as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
Sincerely, Clutch
